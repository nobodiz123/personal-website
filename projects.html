About Me

Hey, I'm Noel, a student at LASA High School in Austin, Texas. In my free time, I enjoy working on ciphers and solving real world problems.

My mission is to use computer science as an application to solve problems in different disciplines. I believe that we must use the current technology to first solve our inward systemic inequalities before venturing outward to discover the science of the future. Combining my knowledge in computer science tools and wet lab research, I have worked toward this goal by creating cheaper and accessible molecular diagnostic assays that function more efficiently. In my free time, I enjoy competing in computer science challenged like Google Kickstart , the National Cyberpatriots Competition - Gold, and running.

My current interests lie in using machine learning to analyze empirical data and predict human behaviors with deep neural networks. I am interested in combine my knowledge in data science and cognitive science in the developing field of neuro symbolic AI. Additionally, I am interested in security - especially homomorphic encryption and the mathematical theory behind it.
